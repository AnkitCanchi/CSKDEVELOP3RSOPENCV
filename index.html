Tutorials

Drop these into your notes and update the details to match your camera and field setup.

1) Pipeline Checklist

  1. Pick one target: sleeve, prop, tag, or colored element.
  2. Lock exposure and resolution settings.
  3. Add overlay telemetry: FPS, threshold values, target X/Y.
  4. Test at 3 distances: close, mid, far.
  5. Retest under different lighting and from different angles.

2) Parameter Tracking

# Vision Parameter Log (copy this) date: camera: resolution: exposure: target: - hsv_lower: [H,S,V] - hsv_upper: [H,S,V] - min_area: - max_area: - roi: results: - detection_rate: - avg_fps: - notes:

3) AprilTag Sanity Checks

  • Confirm camera intrinsics match your resolution.
  • Measure 1 real distance and compare to estimated distance.
  • Test at different angles, not only straight-on.
  • Log detection FPS to avoid lag in autonomous.

4) Competition Prep

  • Bring final tuning values in a printed or offline note.
  • Have a no-vision backup autonomous routine ready.
  • Check camera mount, cable strain relief, and focus.
  • Run 10+ practice starts before queueing.

FAQ

Quick answers for when vision becomes inconsistent.

Lighting changes, background clutter, and different camera exposure can break thresholds. Re-test under bright lights and add a safety margin to your filters.
Use an ROI, filter by contour area, and average results over a few frames. Keep FPS consistent by avoiding heavy drawing inside the pipeline.
